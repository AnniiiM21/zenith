Privacy Policy for Zenith - Peak Productivity Timer & Focus Tracker

Last Updated: December 2024

Overview

Zenith is a productivity extension that helps users track their focus sessions and analyze their browsing patterns to improve productivity. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

Key Point: All data collected by Zenith stays locally on your device. We never send any data to external servers or third parties.

Data Collection & Usage

What We Collect

Zenith collects the following data locally on your device only:

  • Website Visit Data: Domain names (e.g., "github.com"), page titles, time spent, visit counts
  • Browser Activity: Tab switching frequency, window focus changes, user engagement indicators
  • Timer Data: Focus session durations, break times, session types, timer preferences

Why We Need These Permissions

  • "Access your data on all websites" - Track time spent across all sites for productivity analytics
  • "Read your browsing history" - Detect tab switches to calculate accurate time tracking
  • "Display notifications" - Alert you when timer sessions complete

Data Storage & Security

Local Storage Only

  • All data stays on your device using Chrome's secure local storage
  • No personal information is transmitted over the internet
  • No user accounts or cloud sync required
  • Data is automatically cleaned up (last 50 sessions kept)

What We DON'T Collect

  • Personal information (name, email, etc.)
  • Passwords or sensitive form data
  • File contents or downloads
  • Specific URLs (only domain names)
  • Data from incognito/private browsing sessions

Your Control & Rights

  • Start/Stop Tracking: You control when tracking is active
  • Clear Data: Remove all stored data through extension popup
  • View Data: See all collected data through the Analytics tab
  • Disable Extension: Uninstall anytime to stop all data collection

Technical Implementation

Our content script runs on web pages to detect user engagement and measure time spent. The extension works completely offline with no external communication.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices. Updates will be posted with extension updates and on our GitHub repository.
